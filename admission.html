Admission


IIIT Kota currently offers the following programmes.

1) 4-year B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering (student strength 60)

2) 4-year B. Tech. in Electronics and Communications Engineering (student strength 60)

For details on IIIT Kota institute code and branch codes, see institute profile page on JoSAA website.

Admissions


All admissions into B. Tech. program (Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering) at IIIT Kota are through the process as announced by CSAB.

Classes

The classes for IIIT Kota are being held at MNIT Jaipur campus (http://www.mnit.ac.in). For the schedule of classes and examinations, see Time Table page.

Accommodation

The students admitted to B. Tech. program of IIIT Kota may be accommodated in the hostel premises of MNIT Jaipur as per the availability of hostel rooms. The final decision on the availability of accommodation (for both boys and girls) and on the revision of hostel fee, lies with the mentor institute MNIT Jaipur.
