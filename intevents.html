IIIT Kota celebrated its foundation day


IIIT Kota celebrated its first ever foundation day from March 31 to April 1, 2017. IIIT Kota students participated in various events over these two days and it was covered by the press as well.



At inauguration function, institute’s mentor director advised students to aim higher in life and to enjoy the events. It was mentioned that IIIT Kota is among the top three institutes of its kind out of fifteen such institutes established under PPP mode.



IIIT Kernel and inaugural lecture


IIIT Kota launched its technical forum named ‘IIIT Kernel‘ on March 11, 2017. Through this forum, the institute plans to make outreach to leaders in the industry as well as academia.



Dr. Giriraj Nyati, Chief Operating Officer, Secure Meters Ltd. delivered inaugural lecture of IIIT Kernel on March 24, 2017 on “Doing business in the today’s environment” at Neeti Sabhagaar, 2nd Floor, Prabha Bhavan.



IIIT Kota at Gusto-2017, IIITDM Jabalpur



Students of IIIT Kota participated in Gusto 2017 held at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM), Jabalpur during January 27-29, 2017.


Total 38 students participated in various games (Athletics, Badminton, Chess, Cricket, Table Tennis and Carrom).

68th Republic Day Celebration


IIIT Kota celebrated 68th Republic Day with mentor institute MNIT Jaipur. Director Prof. Uday Kumar Yaragatti addressed students, faculty and staff. He motivated students to work for excellence and towards the betterment of the country.





Visitors from France



Dr. Mohamed Mosbah and Dr. Akka Zemari, Professors from University of Bordeaux visited IIIT Kota in January 2017. Dr. Mosbah provided insight into admissions at French University. Dr. Akka Zemari delivered a lecture on Machine Learning algorithms.



IIIT Kota stands 3rd overall at Inter IIIT Sports Meet – 2016

Team IIIT Kota ranked 3rd overall and institute is proud of this achievement.
