Annapurna Canteen


The campus has a couple of food joints, frequented more perhaps by hostelites looking for a change from hostel grub than the occasional visitor.

The Campus Canteen is open from 8.30 A.M. to 8.00 P.M. on all week days. A wide variety of South Indian and North Indian dishes are available.

A Food Point located in Hostel 4 is open from 9.00 A.M. to 1.00 A.M. A variety of Indian dishes are available here.
