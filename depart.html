Departments

Computer Science And Engineering


Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Kota started functioning from July 2013.

Currently, the department offers a four-year course leading to Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. The department is benefited from distinguished faculty of CSE department at MNIT Jaipur, our mentor institute. Department is in the process to get state-of-the-art labs to support the undergraduate program better.

The department is benefited from distinguished faculty of ECE department at MNIT Jaipur, our mentor institute.
